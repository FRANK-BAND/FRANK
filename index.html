About

Who is F.R.A.N.K?

Freedom of Riff And Noise Kooky is a trio hard-rock band from Bandung, Indonesia. Filled by Joseph “Deblo” Jr as guitar, vocal, “Bobby” El as bass, vocal, and Rams “Feardost” as drum. In this moment, the band has release the new single from the album that has not been recorded. Proudly, we present our single, Discourse.
